Dragon Ball FighterZ

Overview

Dragon Ball FighterZ is a competitive 2.5D fighting game that delivers high-speed action, stunning visuals, and iconic Dragon Ball Z characters.

Features

  • Developed by Arc System Works
  • 3v3 Team Battles
  • Accessible for newcomers and deep for competitive players

Game Modes

Story Mode

Follow a unique storyline where the Z Fighters face off against an army of androids, including the mysterious Android 21.

Online Battles

Compete against players worldwide in ranked or casual matches, testing your skills against the best.

Popular Characters

  • Goku (Super Saiyan Blue)
  • Frieza (Golden Form)
  • Cell (Perfect Form)

Dragon Ball Z: Kakarot

Overview

Dragon Ball Z: Kakarot is an open-world action RPG where players relive the epic story of Goku and his friends, complete with iconic battles and heartfelt moments.

Game Highlights

  • Explore the world of Dragon Ball Z
  • Engage in epic boss fights
  • Experience heartfelt side quests

Playable Sagas

Saiyan Saga

Face off against Raditz, Nappa, and Vegeta in a battle for Earth's survival.

Namek Saga

Journey to Planet Namek and battle the tyrant Frieza in his various forms.

Majin Buu Saga

Confront Majin Buu's transformations and unlock Goku's ultimate potential.

Customization

  • Level up characters and abilities
  • Unlock transformations like Super Saiyan
  • Cook meals and gather resources to boost stats
